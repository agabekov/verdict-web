FAQs

A few of the questions film lovers ask before they make Verdict part of their routine.

How personal are recommendations?

The app trains an on-device Foundation ML model once you log a handful of ratings. You can see which strategy (heuristic, hybrid, or Core ML) delivered each pick.

Do watchlists sync across devices?

Yes. Verdict uses CloudKit and a local-first approach, so every change is saved offline and synced the moment you're back online.

Can I follow streaming availability in my country?

Absolutely. Provider lists respect your region, and you can filter by stream, rent, or buy—plus receive release notifications.

Is Verdict available worldwide?

Verdict supports 43 languages and localized TMDb content. App Store availability expands as we complete regional compliance.

How does Verdict make money?

Through optional donations, Verdict+ subscriptions, and thoughtfully placed AdMob banners that respect GDPR consent.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Verdict+ subscribers can generate formatted exports for ratings and watchlists, perfect for sharing or backup.
